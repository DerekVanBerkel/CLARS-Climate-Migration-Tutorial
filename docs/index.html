Welcome

This website is a hub for CLARS trainings, background materials, datasets, schedules, and instructions for using the PIVOT model. It supports both the online training and the in-person engagements:

  • Online training: Feb 3, 2026 (3:00–5:00 EAT)

  • Arusha, Tanzania: Feb 9–10, 2026 (9:00–17:00 EAT)

  • Kisii, Kenya: Feb 12–13, 2026 (9:00–17:00 EAT)

  • Mbarara, Uganda: Feb 17–18, 2026 (9:00–17:00 EAT)

This training series aims to support the exploration of climate risk, migration, and urban planning as part of the CLARS project (Understanding and Adapting to Climate Migration in the Great Lakes and Lake Victoria Regions). Using open data and reproducible R workflows, you willl work through city examples and adapt them for your local context.

This site includes:

  • Short tutorials and example analyses for Mwanza, Tanzania, Eldoret, Kampala, Uganda, and example analyses (R Markdown) you can adapt to your city
  • Background on climate migration concepts, drivers, and datasets
  • The training Training Schedule and background Literature

Who this is for

  • Practitioners and analysts in planning, public works, resilience, and sustainability
  • Students and researchers collaborating with cities on climate adaptation

What you will learn

  • Frame climate migration: Use spatial evidence to support discussion in CLARS regional meetings.
  • Work with city data in R: Load, clean, and visualize buildings, hazards, and demographics.
  • Plan with PIVOT: Explore data, analyze future growth scenarios, and create actionable plans—plus get “train-the-trainer” skills to teach others in your region. Pivot-Model
